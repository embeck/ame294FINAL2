About

My name is Erin Beck, and I've been drawing for as long as I remember. I play more video games than I have time for, and make more costumes than I can afford. Currently, I am going to school for Digital Culture and Film at Arizona State University, and am interning at Walt Disney World Resort this Fall. I am looking to become an animator, or just make up stories and characters for a living.

Projects

My various works from my portfolios and projects.

Godspeed

Original Webcomic

On a search for adventure, a boy named Traverse finds a pocketwatch possessed by the soul of the Greek warrior Icarus. After a fateful night, he finds himself thrown into a world of gods and magic.

Monochrome Kingdom

Original Roleplaying Game

In a cruel twist of fate, Shiro wakes up in the middle of the train tracks with no memories and a message reading "Praise Deus." In order to survive in this strange urban afterlife, he must befriend and fight alongside both angels and demons. Because, in this world, the line isn't clear.

Final Fallacy

Fan Webcomic

Based on Square Enix's "Final Fantasy" video game series. Noctis is from the yet-to-be-released title "Final Fantasy XV." He's been in development hell for nearly 9 years, but the other members that have made Tokyo their summer home have made the wait more bearable.
